Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 3, 2026

This policy explains how The Retrofit Garage handles data in its TikTok automation service.

1. Data We Collect

When you authorize our TikTok app, we receive from TikTok:

  • Access token (short-lived, ~24h)
  • Refresh token (long-lived, ~365 days)
  • Open ID (unique TikTok user identifier)
  • User info (username, display name, profile picture URL)

Additionally:

  • Video files you upload for posting
  • Post metadata (captions, hashtags, visibility settings)

2. How We Use Data

  • Upload videos to your authorized TikTok account
  • Refresh access tokens automatically to maintain service
  • Check upload status and retry if needed
  • Generate logs for debugging and audit

3. Data Storage

All data is stored on a private Linux server:

  • TikTok tokens are stored in /root/.openclaw/workspace/secrets/tiktok-api.json with permissions 600 (owner read/write only).
  • Video files are stored temporarily in USER/Business/tiktok-videos/ until uploaded, then deleted.
  • Logs are written to logs/tiktok-*.log (rotated periodically). Logs do NOT contain access tokens or refresh tokens.
  • Server is protected by firewall; only authorized processes can access the secrets/ directory.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with:

  • Any third parties (marketing, analytics, etc.)
  • Other users or services

We only communicate with TikTok's official API endpoints (open.tiktokapis.com) to perform the upload.

5. Data Retention

  • Access tokens are kept until revocation or expiration; we refresh automatically.
  • Refresh tokens are kept indefinitely but can be revoked via TikTok.
  • Uploaded videos are removed from our server after successful upload (within minutes).
  • Logs are retained for 30 days then deleted.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a summary of stored data (contact email below).
  • Deletion: Revoke our app's access via TikTok settings (see below).
  • Portability: Export your data by requesting the tiktok-api.json file (we can send it securely).

7. Revoking Access

To stop the service:

  1. Open TikTok app on your phone
  2. Go to Profile → Settings and privacy (≡ menu)
  3. Select Security and login
  4. Tap See authorized apps
  5. Find "The Retrofit Garage TikTok Manager" and tap Remove

Revocation is immediate; we will no longer be able to post on your behalf.

8. AI-Generated Content Disclosure

If any video content is generated or edited using AI tools, we will disclose it in the video description or overlay as required by TikTok's policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service does not target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. The "Effective Date" above indicates the latest version. Check this page periodically.
